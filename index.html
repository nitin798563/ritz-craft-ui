About Us

With Us, It’s Personal

We’re the largest family-owned, off-site, modular home manufacturer in the United States. Although our company is large in size, our focus is on the finest details of your home. To continue delivering on our promises, we operate under three guiding principles:

  • Providing a safe and productive work environment for our employees
  • Developing strong, decade-long relationships with our independent builder network
  • Providing products and services to home buyers that are unparalleled in design, workmanship, and quality
